Parallel

The powerful Ostodar siblings are one mistake away from the destruction of their entire family.

Every person in the city of Falsk is a risk, including each member of their family. And when two strangers show up from a far away land, it complicates things even further.

Zephyra and Davian arrive on a mission to find valuable information that could help save their entire kingdom—a land steeped in the magic and ferocious beasts that are now only legends to the cut-throat society of Falsk. Their best hope at tracking down the information they so vitally need comes through attending a high society party thrown by a musician new to the deceptive nobility. In Falsk, these parties are where plots are hatched, alliances are formed, and schemes are carried out, all to benefit the Councilmen and their powerful families.

The Ostodars are keeping secrets they'sd prefer remain hidden, and when the two strangers are thrown into their world, some siblings take them as a threat, and some an opportunity. Zephyra and Davian must navigate their unfamiliar world or find themselves pulled too deeply into it.

Told from multiple overlapping perspectives over the course of four action-packed days, Parallel will transport you to a world of intrigue, tragedy, romance, and deception, where complex decisions could save or ruin lives, and selflessness and greed are in constant battle.
