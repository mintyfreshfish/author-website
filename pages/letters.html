Letters

Zephyra and Davian’s hasty departure from Falsk left everyone involved with unsaid goodbyes and unspoken sentiments. Beginning just weeks after the exciting conclusion of Parallel, this series of letters, from Falsk to Raven’s Port and back, chronicles the continuing friendships--and, in some cases, enmity--between well-loved characters. Join Zephyra, Davian, the Ostodars, Skeigi, Drotan, and some new friends as well, as they continue their story through correspondence.
