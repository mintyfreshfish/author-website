Da-Ae-1


6 Tor.

Aenna,

You really think I‘d stoop low enough to accept anything from you, especially after what you did? No amount of gold would convince me to help you with anything. Ever. Enclosed you‘ll find your fifty gold pieces. Though forgive me if I didn‘t seal the pouch tightly enough. You know how these lower class tradesmen are. They‘ll steal anything they can get their hands on. You should probably send them to the gallows for it.

I hope you rot in the Abyss,

Also, escaped Falsk without incident? Really? You mean aside from being falsely accused of murder and almost hanged? Or maybe you forgot all about that in the midst of your overly-dramatic recovery while Freir fawned all over you. I hope he rots in the Abyss with you. You deserve each other.

You know, you deserve much worse than that. Rotting in the Abyss would be too kind for you. I think I'd add in some purple worm poison, but just enough you'll live for a month in utter agony. And let's include starvation too while we're at it. You sounded thrilled about bestowing that on Selos, so how about we throw it right back at you? Madness? What else can I add to the list? You deserve them all. Every single one. And I'll let the world know what a murderous witch you really are. There's that publicity you're always talking about.
