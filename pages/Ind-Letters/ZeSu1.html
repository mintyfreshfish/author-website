Ze-Su-1


Dear Sura,

I‘m writing on behalf of a friend who wished to tell you she is so very sorry she was unable to tell you goodbye and to thank you for all you did to help her during her stay in Falsk. She knows it was inconvenient, and yet you went out of your way to make her feel welcome, despite less than ideal circumstances. She appreciates that so very much, and she wishes with all her heart she could tell you that in person.

Things are not always as they seem, and she was grateful to make a friend who believed the best in people, rather than what others said about them. She hopes you can find it in your heart to continue to do the same for her.

She hopes more than anything that this letter somehow finds you in a brief moment of joy. Inclosed is a delicious tea from the faraway city of Raven‘s Port. My dear friend hopes you will be able to manage a few minutes for yourself to relax and enjoy it.

With all her heart, she hopes you, and your siblings, and everyone else in Falsk, is well. She would love to hear all the news there, as she misses them all tremendously. Please, please write back. Thank you again for everything.

Much Love,
