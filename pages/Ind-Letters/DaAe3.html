Da-Ae-3


Uncultured Vixen,

Your letter took a little longer to reach me this time. Unfortunately your servant or messenger was found dead in one of the tunnels. Couldn’t even get past his first Fivul Beast. I hope he was one of your favorites. Unlucky for me, a passing traveler picked up the letter after he raided the pockets of your servant and took all the gold I’m guessing you paid him. So the letter found me after all. I was really hoping I was rid of you for good.

I’ve already taken down two of the hunters you sent my way. Don’t worry. Despite all your talk, I know you actually want me alive, and I’ll make sure I stay that way. Just for you.

Zephyra sends her love for some reason. We’ve been seeing each other more since I’m frequently back in Raven’s Port. Lucky for me she is naively forgiving and has always been a much better kisser than you.

Find someone else’s head for Torin’s dogs. Mine is actually useful attached, unlike Freir’s. Perhaps he’d be a better alternative for decapitation.

Affectionately Yours,
